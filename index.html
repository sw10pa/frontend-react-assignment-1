What I Learned from the First Lecture

Headings and Text Formatting

There are 6 headings from h1 to h6, and paragraphs are used for regular text.

b and strong both make text bold, but strong has more semantic importance for search engines. Similarly, i and em italicize text, with em indicating emphasis. Additionally, u underlines text, small makes it smaller, and mark highlights it.

Math and Lists

Subscripts and superscripts are used with sub and sup tags, respectively.

Example: H2O and 23 = 8.

An unordered list:

  • First item;
  • Second item;
  • Third item.

An ordered list:

  1. First item;
  2. Second item;
  3. Third item.
